Frequently Asked Questions

Is it completely safe for direct skin application?
Yes. Our attar oils are 100% alcohol-free and formulated with pure botanical extracts and sandalwood base oil that protect and soothe sensitive skin.
Will the oils leave stain marks on white clothes?
Because attars are oil-concentrates, direct application on delicate light fabrics can leave wet spots. We recommend dabbing directly onto your skin's pulse points.
