Centuries of Perfumery

The Heritage of Kannauj

In the heart of Uttar Pradesh lies Kannauj, India's perfume capital, where time has stood still. For centuries, artisans have captured the scent of wet clay and fresh blooms using copper stills (Degs) and clay condensers (Bhapkas).

Each drop of Mahekh attar is crafted via this traditional steam-distillation process, fusing natural botanical vapors directly into pure sandalwood oil base. It is a slow, spiritual art—resulting in a rich, soul-stirring fragrance that synthetic sprays can never replicate.

Scent Preservation

How To Apply

1

Apply on Pulse Points

Dab attar on wrists, inner elbows, neck, and behind ears. Body heat in these zones diffuses fragrance naturally.

2

Dab, Do Not Rub

Lightly dab wrists together. Never rub them vigorously, as friction breaks down delicate top note scent molecules.

3

Moisturize for Longevity

Apply an unscented lotion or moisturizer on pulse points before dabbing attar. Hydrated skin locks scent longer.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How is Mahekh attar different from ordinary spray perfumes?
Our attars are 100% pure concentrated fragrance oils base-filled with sandalwood, completely free from ethyl alcohol and water. Ordinary spray perfumes contain up to 90% alcohol which evaporates rapidly in 3 hours. Mahekh attars react with your body heat, diffusing a long-lasting scent trail for 12+ hours.
Is concentrated attar safe for sensitive skin?
Yes. Since our attar blends are alcohol-free and use natural botanical essences inside pure carrier oils, they do not cause skin drying, irritation, or redness commonly associated with alcohol sprays. However, we always recommend doing a small patch test on your wrist first.
Will the oils stain my light-colored clothes?
Because attar is oil-based, applying directly to delicate, light fabrics (like silk or white linen) can leave temporary oil rings. We recommend applying attar directly to your skin's pulse points. If you want to scent your clothes, apply it inside seams or underneath collars where it won't show.
