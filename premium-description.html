Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe for daily skin application?
Yes. Our attar oils are 100% alcohol-free and formulated with organic sandalwood carrier oils which protect and hydrate sensitive skin.
Will the attar oils stain light clothing?
Applying pure oil on delicate fabrics can leave temporary wet marks. We recommend dabbing directly onto your skin (pulse points) for safe and deep projection.
