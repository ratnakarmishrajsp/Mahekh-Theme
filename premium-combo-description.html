Legacy of Kannauj

Traditional Deg-Bhapka Steam Craft

Every bottle of attar in this combo pack is distilled using the ancient Deg & Bhapka steam hydro-distillation system in Kannauj. The petals, woods, and raw materials are placed in large copper pots (Degs) sealed with clay, connected via bamboo pipes to water-filled cooling vessels (Bhapkas).

As firewood warms the Deg, fragrant steam travels through the bamboo pipes to diffuse directly into a receiver filled with pure Sandalwood carrier oil. It takes weeks of skilled labor and constant fire monitoring to bottle just a single batch. No synthetic chemicals, no pressure cookers—just absolute purity preserved across generations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers From Our Experts

Which fragrances are included in this pack of 3 combo set?
The combo set contains three premium 10ml bottles: Kesar Chandan (sweet saffron blended with sandalwood), Kasturi (majestic, deep, and royal dry-wood musk), and Rose (fresh, romantic, dewy rose). You receive a total of 30ml of pure, concentrated, alcohol-free premium attar oil.
How long do these attars last on the body?
Because these attars are highly concentrated, 100% pure oil formulations without any alcohol, they last for 12+ hours on your pulse points. They anchor to your skin and slow-release their aromatic notes through your body heat. When applied to clothes, the fragrance can linger for days.
Are these attars safe for daily use on sensitive skin?
Absolutely. Our traditional attars are alcohol-free, paraben-free, and chemical-free. Distilled gently inside traditional copper pots and blended on a pure sandalwood oil base, they are soothing, moisturizing, and completely safe for all skin types.
Will the attar oils stain my clothes?
Since these are oil-based, it is best to apply them directly onto your skin (wrists, neck, behind ears). If you wish to apply them to clothes, dab a small amount onto your palms, rub gently, and swipe across your garments, avoiding direct contact with light-colored or delicate silks.
